2026 Wales Senedd Election

The 2026 Senedd election in Wales will be the first conducted under a new proportional representation system. Wales has been divided into 16 constituencies, each of which will elect 6 members to the Senedd.

Hexmap - fixed area hexmap

There are 2 hexmaps here. The first uses a single hexagon to represent each constituency, with each MS represented by a triangle covering 1/6 of the hexagon. The second uses 6 hexagons to represent each constituency, with one MS per hexagon.
